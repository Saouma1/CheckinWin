FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Checkinwin is a mobile app that offers a loyalty program for various businesses and categories. It allows you to discover new businesses, earn rewards for your loyalty, and engage with your favorite places.

Once you download the Checkinwin app, you can search for participating businesses in your area. When you visit a business, simply check in through the app to earn points or rewards. You can track your progress, discover exclusive prizes, and redeem your rewards through the app.

You can earn rewards by checking in at participating businesses. Each check-in will earn you points that can be redeemed for exclusive discounts, gifts or other rewards offered by the businesses. The more you visit and engage with the businesses, the more rewards you can win.

The Checkinwin app is available for use in various cities and locations. You can explore and discover participating businesses in your area through the app.

Yes, the Checkinwin app is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden charges or subscription fees. Simply download the app, sign up for an account, and start enjoying the benefits of the loyalty program.
